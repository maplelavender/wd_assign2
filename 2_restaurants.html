Palermo excels in Argentinian cooking techniques, experienced at their best when sampling premium meats cooked atop the glowing hot coals of Melbourne’s first Asado fire pit, in a marriage with their classic Parrilla (charcoal grill).

Palermo sources only the highest quality, locally raised, pasture-fed Hereford and Angus beef. The majority of, coming from O’Connor’s Premium, the very best beef from Gippsland, if not the world.
